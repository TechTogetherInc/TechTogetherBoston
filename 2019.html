Explore with Us

TechTogether Boston is one the world’s largest all-female and femme non-binary hackathons. For 36 hours, we equip women with all the resources they need to transform their dreams into reality.

No experience is necessary to attend TechTogether Boston, only a passion for exploring the exciting the world of technology.


What is a hackathon?


You’ll enjoy a weekend of awesome tech, food, swag, workshops, and mini-events while learning from distinguished speakers, inspiring mentors, and peers.


Transform Ideas into Impact

Hackathons can be an intimidating environment for anyone involved (even us organizers), so we have curated special tracks to help you get the most out of our event.

See one or more track that you like? No problem, we welcome women who want to do it all and drink some soylent too.
