FAQ

How do I register?

You can register for TechTogether with the form here! We are accepting applicants on a rolling basis until late December.

Where and when is TechTogether?

TechTogether will be held in Agganis Arena on March 22-24, 2019.

How much will it cost to attend?

This event, including all food, swag, and prizes, is completely free!

Who can attend?

We welcome female and femme non-binary individuals of all skill levels who have a passion for learning more about technology and are 16 years or older.
