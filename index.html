Our Mission

At TechTogether Boston, we're looking to shape the way women get involved with tech.

We aim create an inclusive environment to encourage more underrepresented people to either get introduced to the world of technology or harness their skills to create projects of their own.

With the support of the technology community, industry, and individuals we can chip away at the gender gap and empower anyone from someone with a non-technical major with an interest in tech to seasoned hackathon veterans looking to build out a complicated, groundbreaking product with a little extra support.

What is a hackathon?


You'll enjoy a weekend of awesome tech, food, swag, workshops, and mini-events while learning from distinguished speakers, inspiring mentors, and peers.

Explore with Us

TechTogether Boston is one the world's largest all-female and femme non-binary hackathons. For 36 hours, we equip women with all the resources they need to transform their dreams into reality.

No experience is necessary to attend TechTogether Boston, only a passion for exploring the exciting the world of technology!

Community Spotlight

Wondering what kind of projects you'll get to work on? Here are some of our favorites from SheHacks 2018!
Puppy Time

Entrepreneurial Hack Winner

Platform to help shelters with donations and adoption by connecting people with a live stream of adorable shelter pups! Made by a beginner team!

Ladies in Technology

SOCIAL IMPACT HACK WINNER

Empowering app to make it easier for women to find professional connections! L.I.T. can help grow your network, share ideas, and make new friends.

The Final Meltdown

Virtual Reality Hack Winner

VR app that simulates icebergs melting all around you as a way to see the negative effects of climate change. Made by a beginner team!
