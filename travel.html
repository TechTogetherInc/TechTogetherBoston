Car directions_car

Gas will be reimbursed for any car that has 3 or more hackers in it. To qualify as a hacker, each person must be: registered, checked in during the event, and have submitted a project to DevPost during the judging period.

Please check the chart to see how much of your gas we may be able to cover. We have limited funds, so reimbursement may be provided on a first-come-first-serve basis.

Distance from Boston (roundtrip) Maximum Reimbursement (roundtrip)
0~50 miles $0
50~100 miles Up to $20
100~200 miles Up to $40
200~300 miles Up to $60
300~400 miles Up to $80
400+ miles Up to $100

Other Inquiries directions_subway flight

We may be able to reimburse you for other modes of transportation. Please check the chart to see what you may be qualified for. All these requests will be considered on a case-by-case basis. In order to be reimbursed, you must:

  • 1. Retain your proof of purchase for flight/train/bus tickets
  • 2. Submit your project to our DevPost
  • 3. Fill out a reimbursement form after the hackathon is over

We will not be able to reimburse you if you don't fulfill all the requirements
