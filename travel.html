Travel Reimbursement

Bus

Buses will depart from New York City, NY, and Princeton, NJ for the event. If you are not near either of these locations and are interested in having a bus come to your school, email us at info@techtogether.io and we can discuss the possibility of a bus coming to a location near you.

Car

If your school is not along the bus route, you may still be considered for travel reimbursement. Gas will be reimbursed for any car that has 3 or more hackers in it (to qualify as a hacker, each person must be registered, checked in during the event, and have submitted a project to DevPost during the judging period).

Please check the chart to see how much of your gas we may be able to cover. We have limited funds, so reimbursement may be provided on a first-come-first-serve basis.

Distance from Boston (roundtrip) Maximum Reimbursement (roundtrip)
0~50 miles $0
50~100 miles $20
100~200 miles $40
200~300 miles $60
300~400 miles $80
400+ miles $100
